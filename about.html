About Me

I bring a unique skill set to designing user experience, drawing from my background as a copy editor and journalist, where I learned the importance of clarity, consistency and collaboration, and applied my natural curiosity about people to question WHY they do what they do. My curiosity for people and cultures eventually led me to Spain, then Sweden, where I have lived since 2008. Over a decade of experience as a passionate educator and curriculum designer for language learners has made inclusivity and accessibility second nature to me.

Aside from being a UX designer, I am also a...

Photographer · Traveler · Writer · Curriculum designer · White space enthusiast · Pop culture junkie · Amateur genealogist · Coffee aficionado · Cat content creator · Polyglot · Trivia-night winner
